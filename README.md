# Risk Radar

Risk Radar is an AI-powered browser extension that helps users identify hidden risks in financial websites, privacy policies, and terms & conditions.

The platform simplifies complex legal and financial language into understandable risk summaries, helping users make safer digital decisions.

---

# Problem Statement

Most users accept financial platform policies without reading or understanding the hidden legal implications.

Important clauses related to:
- data sharing
- liability waivers
- arbitration
- tracking permissions
- fraud responsibility

are often buried inside lengthy terms & conditions.

Risk Radar aims to make these risks visible and understandable in seconds.

---

# Solution

Risk Radar scans website content and generates:
- Risk summaries
- Clause-based analysis
- Financial/privacy warnings
- Quick safety checks
- User-friendly explanations

The extension is designed to make legal and financial policies easier to understand for everyday users.

2. Risk Radar scans website content
3. AI analyzes legal and financial clauses
4. Risk categories are generated
5. User receives simplified risk insights
